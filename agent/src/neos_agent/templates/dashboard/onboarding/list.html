Universal Agreement Field (UAF)

The UAF is the root agreement every member consents to. It defines the shared foundation of the ecosystem through 6 sections:

1. Purpose & Values

Shared purpose and core value commitments

2. Governance Structure

Authority distribution and domains

3. Decision-Making

ACT process and escalation

4. Resource Agreements

Economic and Current-See management

5. Conflict Resolution

Escalation and repair processes

6. Exit Rights

Voluntary exit and data portability
