Agreement fields help define and support specific values and principles. Space agreements are not covered here, and can be written for specific spaces. These agreement fields are specifically for the organization. We understand that if we are a part of an Ecosystem, that we have embodied that Ecosystem’s agreement fields as well as specific agreements that maintain our structural integrity within our ETHOS.

## **Agreements and Accountability**

- I agree to honor all agreements I enter into, including those regarding shared access to information, tools, and platforms.
- I will not knowingly lie or use false information to manipulate others. I am here to be a part of a mutually beneficial world.
- I agree to gently remind members of these agreements when they are not being adhered to, and I will respectfully reflect when I receive such reminders myself.
- I agree that any member, including myself, that does not or cannot adhere to these agreements after gentle reminders and mediation, can be removed from their role and lose organizational access temporarily or permanently.
- I agree to follow the [ACT process](https://www.notion.so/Decisions-64553e1130a349238d60e6a12c0a4a9a?pvs=21) for any modifications to ETHOS agreements. These must reach consensus.
- If I need to relocate to a new role, I will follow the agreed-upon [relocation processes](https://www.notion.so/Circles-Roles-9305b9bc10fb45f3aabdeacf7ec6d594?pvs=21).
- I agree to consider my own needs and capacity when contributing, to avoid overload and burnout.
- I will keep my team informed and honor my commitments. I will do what I said I would do, when I said I would do it.
- If I doubt my ability to fulfill a task or timeline related to my role, I agree to notify affected members and spaces to seek coverage and ensure group goals don’t suffer.
    - Transparency in communication such as our intentions, expectations, and abilities is essential.

## **Processes**

- I agree to honor others’ time, show up to meetings I agreed to be in, and be [responsible](https://www.notion.so/New-Paradigm-Encyclopedia-62c58022c75a48f49bf69874bd2dc62f?pvs=21) when I fall short of this.
    - Meetings and events have agreed-upon protocols. While attendance is not mandatory, I will follow defined protocols for meetings I show up to. I may access recordings or find alternative solutions should I not align with a certain meeting facilitation style or process.
- I agree to utilize the dedicated work spaces chosen by the organization for project-related interactions, to keep important information organized and accessible.
- I agree to use existing channels and processes within the ETHOS to propose new initiatives or solutions. If unresolved, I will seek coaching before escalating to the community.
- I agree to utilize consent-based decision-making within organizational work spaces, and to respect the outcome of these collective decisions.
    - See [Moneyless Society Decisions](https://www.notion.so/Decisions-64553e1130a349238d60e6a12c0a4a9a?pvs=21)
- I agree to utilize the proposal process and seek consent to update processes and agreements that I feel are unfair or need improvement.
- During conversations with other members, I agree to respect facilitation guidelines which provide everyone space to speak.
- Timelines support project completion. I will not delay participation in proposals. If I fail to meet a timeline, the project may proceed without me.
- I will seek team support to propose decisions affecting the collective. We choose to move together to remain coherent.

## **Conflict**

- I agree to maintain safety in Moneyless Society spaces by maintaining Mutual Purpose and Mutual Respect
    - **Mutual Purpose** - We have the same goals. We care about each other’s concerns.
    - **Mutual Respect** - We care about each other’s well-being. We remember that each of us is a whole person and not a label to be dismissed.
- If I find myself in conflict with another member, I agree to ask questions and seek understanding before making assumptions, and to re-center if I am off balance.
    - I am solely responsible for my self-care and will not place blame on others for my feelings, or expect them to take responsibility for my well-being.
- If a conflict I am involved in becomes disruptive to the space, I agree to move the conversation to a [mediation](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1hqlMn5zB_u3iAxeXfs2J8Ju66jpbxLxK/edit?pli=1#slide=id.p1) space with available support from the People Circle.
- I agree to request formal mediation if I cannot resolve tensions with another member.
    - If I lose respect for someone’s actions, I will follow Solutionary Culture principles to resolve tensions rather than judge or assume.
        - [Solutionary culture](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1oiuvLWNOY1c6UN_xd1TLfG6NT0EnjEVqNE5cefjSh-s/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.skmu7de8lv1n)
        - [Conflict Resolution Roadmap](https://docs.google.com/file/d/1hqlMn5zB_u3iAxeXfs2J8Ju66jpbxLxK/edit?filetype=mspresentation)
- I agree not to incite [violence](https://www.notion.so/Definition-of-Terms-1c4d14367d5f8075a5f6cb31532579a8?pvs=21) against other people in Moneyless Society spaces
- I agree to refrain from dominating other members with behaviors like insults, threats, and manipulation.
- I may make others aware of my personal triggers and traumas, but agree not to make them responsible for my responses or emotional well-being.
- I agree to encourage respectful discussion by giving others a chance to respond and ask questions. I will accept feedback with curiosity and thoughtful integration.
- I agree to speak about others in a supportive and relevant manner, whether they are present or not.
- Everyone has the right to choose who they work with. If I cannot work with my team, I agree to relocate or find a new role. If I do not have a role, my co-creator status and access may be affected.
- If a team finds it too difficult to work with a member after mediation attempts, they may agree to remove that member. The removed member must find a new role to maintain access to the ETHOS.
    - Refer to [Relocation Process](https://www.notion.so/Circles-Roles-9305b9bc10fb45f3aabdeacf7ec6d594?pvs=21)

## **Stewardship & Contribution**

- The ETHOS will provide basic needs for all full contributors at Stage 2 of the [Funds and Distribution plan](https://www.notion.so/Funds-and-Distribution-1afd14367d5f808781bbd68d9292b47a?pvs=21). Anyone working 20 hours a week or more for an ETHOS is considered a full contributor. However, I understand that a forming ETHOS may not have sufficient income or assets, and I will prioritize my well-being before ETHOS needs. The ETHOS can assist, but it is ultimately my responsibility.
- I will not divert funds from collective funding pools into personal accounts or assets outside agreed-upon expenditures. I may receive agreed-upon resources but will not send funds to non-approved sources. All spending must be backed by an approved proposal or team agreement.
- I commit to learning and embodying effective stewardship practices. This means as an official steward, I will keep up to date with any final approved stewardship training approved by the ETHOS I am stewarding for.
- When acting as a Steward of a space, I will post all space agreements in accessible locations.
    - Space agreements may not be changed without the consent of all involved.
- I will clearly label “original works”. These are defined as any work that I completed on my own that I wish to receive credit for. I will select appropriate use of original works within any ETHOS that I am working within for these spaces to use and clearly communicate how they should be used.
    - “Emergent works” (defined as a combination of multiple contributors’ original works) will be co-stewarded by the parts of the Ecosystem or ETHOS that utilize those emergent works.
    - See these [Omni License options](https://www.notion.so/OMNI-Licenses-Options-21edf82b042142efaf9dc9248d47ff1d?pvs=21) for ideas on how to clearly designate original works.
- An ETHOS may decide to make emergent works non-public. If I take non-public emergent works outside the ETHOS without consent through a passed proposal, I may lose access. Decisions on external collaboration are made collectively.
    - All “shared works” are public and will be 100% transparent.
- I agree to abide by co-creator agreements. This means I will not engage in coercion or blocking behavior. If I do, I may lose access to certain spaces.
    - Review [Moneyless Society Decisions](https://www.notion.so/Decisions-64553e1130a349238d60e6a12c0a4a9a?pvs=21) for more information on blocking behavior

## **Sovereignty and ETHOS Evolution**

- An ETHOS is sovereign and continues beyond individual members. The GenPlan carries its vision and purpose.
- If my ideas do not align with an ETHOS’ GenPlan, I will consider forming a new ETHOS rather than forcing changes.
- ETHOS members may co-create across multiple ETHOS, provided all roles, entry requirements, and agreements are honored.
- When collaborating with non-ETHOS, new agreements will be made to ensure alignment with existing GenPlans and EcoPlans.
- The ETHOS prioritizes insourcing over outsourcing. I will consult resource stewards before external sourcing.
- An ETHOS that privatizes collective assets may lose its status as an ETHOS. ETHOS economic structures follow wealth distribution formulas that rebuild the collective commons and prevent extractive practices.
- An ETHOS financial agreements must align with broader ecosystem stewardship approaches, including [intellectual property agreements](https://www.notion.so/Intellectual-Property-Emergent-Works-dc2d5f57befa43c8b9c8a199080aa968?pvs=21). These will be approved upon entry to the Ecosystem and can only change with consensus.
- ETHOS members must consult lawyers for legal concerns and seek additional guidance when needed.
- Should an ETHOS dissolve, its emergent works can integrate into an existing ecosystem and be freed from the agreements of that specific ETHOS.

## **Sovereignty, Freedom, and Responsibility**

- ETHOS agreements supersede external agreements except where legal codes apply. I acknowledge ETHOS agreements as a private contract outside of state jurisdiction.
- I will not impose my will on others, nor use threats in ETHOS participation.
- I will uphold freedom as a core principle while maintaining integrity and accountability to my agreements.
- If I break a law, I alone am accountable for the legal repercussions. ETHOS and any ecosystem are not responsible for my actions.
