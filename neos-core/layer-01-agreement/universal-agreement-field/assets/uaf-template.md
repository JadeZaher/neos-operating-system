## 1. Agreements and Accountability

I agree to honor all agreements and agreement fields I enter into, including agreements regarding shared access to information, tools, platforms, and spaces.

I agree to communicate truthfully to the best of my ability. I will not knowingly use false information to manipulate others or distort governance processes.

I agree to gently remind fellow participants of these agreements when they are not being honored, and I will reflect thoughtfully when I receive such reminders myself.

I agree that any participant — including myself — who does not honor these agreements after good-faith reminders and mediation may face consequences up to and including removal from roles and temporary or permanent loss of organizational access, as determined through the ecosystem's conflict and repair processes.

I agree to follow the ACT (Advice, Consent, Test) process for any modifications to agreement fields. Changes to this Universal Agreement Field require consensus of the steward council.

I agree to consider my own needs and capacity when contributing, to avoid overload and burnout. I will not sacrifice my wellbeing for the ecosystem, nor will I expect the ecosystem to be responsible for my personal needs beyond what is structurally provided.

I will keep my circles informed and honor my commitments. I will do what I said I would do, when I said I would do it. If I doubt my ability to fulfill a commitment, I agree to notify affected participants promptly so coverage can be arranged.

> **[ECOSYSTEM: OmniOne]** I agree to follow the ACT process for any modifications to ETHOS agreements. These must reach consensus. If I need to relocate to a new role, I will follow the agreed-upon relocation processes. Transparency in communication — including intentions, expectations, and abilities — is essential.

## 2. Processes

I agree to honor others' time and show up to meetings I have committed to attend. When I fall short of this, I take responsibility for the impact.

I agree to use the designated communication channels and workspaces chosen by my circle or AZPO for project-related interactions, keeping important information organized and accessible.

I agree to use existing channels and processes within my AZPO to propose new initiatives or solutions. If unresolved through normal channels, I will seek coaching before escalating to the broader community.

I agree to utilize consent-based decision-making within organizational workspaces, and to respect the outcomes of collective decisions made through legitimate process. I may propose amendments through proper channels but will honor existing decisions while they stand.

I agree to respect facilitation guidelines that ensure everyone has space to speak. I will not dominate discussions or obstruct others' participation.

I understand that timelines support collective progress. I will not delay participation in proposals without communication. If I fail to meet a timeline, the process may proceed without me.

> **[ECOSYSTEM: OmniOne]** Meetings and events have agreed-upon protocols. While attendance is not mandatory, I will follow defined protocols for meetings I attend. I may access recordings or find alternative participation methods if I do not align with a particular facilitation style.

## 3. Conflict

I agree to maintain safety in all ecosystem spaces by upholding Mutual Purpose and Mutual Respect:
- **Mutual Purpose**: We share common goals. We care about each other's concerns.
- **Mutual Respect**: We care about each other's wellbeing. We remember that each person is whole and not a label to be dismissed.

If I find myself in conflict with another participant, I agree to ask questions and seek understanding before making assumptions or judgments, and to re-center myself if I become emotionally dysregulated.

I acknowledge that I am solely responsible for my self-care. I will not place blame on others for my feelings or expect them to take responsibility for my emotional wellbeing.

If a conflict I am involved in becomes disruptive to a shared space, I agree to move the conversation to a mediation space with available support from the relevant support circle.

I agree to request formal mediation if I cannot resolve tensions with another participant through direct dialogue.

I agree not to incite violence against other people in ecosystem spaces. I agree to refrain from dominating others through insults, threats, or manipulation.

I agree to speak about others in a supportive and relevant manner, whether or not they are present. I will accept feedback with curiosity and thoughtful integration.

> **[ECOSYSTEM: OmniOne]** If I lose respect for someone because of their actions, I will follow Solutionary Culture principles to resolve tensions rather than judge or assume. Everyone has the right to choose who they work with — if I cannot work with my team, I agree to seek relocation to a new role.

## 4. Stewardship and Contribution

I understand that the ecosystem strives to provide for full contributors. I also understand that a forming ecosystem may not yet have sufficient resources, and I will prioritize my own wellbeing before ecosystem needs.

I will not divert funds from collective funding pools into personal accounts or assets outside agreed-upon expenditures. All spending must be backed by an approved proposal or circle agreement.

I commit to learning and embodying effective stewardship practices. As a steward of any space or resource, I will keep all relevant agreements posted and accessible.

I will clearly designate my "original works" — creations I completed individually for which I retain credit and specified rights. "Emergent works" (creations combining multiple contributors' original works) are co-stewarded by the parts of the ecosystem that utilize them.

I agree that "shared works" designated as public will maintain full transparency.

I will not take non-public emergent works outside the ecosystem without consent through a properly passed proposal.

> **[ECOSYSTEM: OmniOne]** An ETHOS will provide basic needs for all full contributors at Stage 2 of the Funds and Distribution plan. Anyone working 20 hours a week or more is considered a full contributor. See OMNI License options for designating original works.

## 5. Sovereignty and Evolution

Each AZPO is sovereign and continues beyond individual members. The generative plan (GenPlan) carries its vision and purpose.

If my ideas do not align with my AZPO's GenPlan, I will consider forming a new AZPO rather than forcing changes that contradict the founding vision.

Participants may co-create across multiple AZPOs, provided all roles, entry requirements, and agreements are honored in each.

When collaborating with entities outside the ecosystem, new agreements must be created to ensure alignment with existing plans.

The ecosystem prioritizes insourcing over outsourcing. I will consult resource stewards before seeking external providers.

An AZPO that privatizes collective assets may lose its status within the ecosystem. Economic structures follow wealth distribution principles that rebuild the collective commons and prevent extractive practices.

> **[ECOSYSTEM: OmniOne]** ETHOS members may co-create across multiple ETHOS. ETHOS financial agreements must align with broader ecosystem stewardship approaches, including intellectual property agreements approved upon entry.

## 6. Sovereignty, Freedom, and Responsibility

Ecosystem agreements take precedence over external agreements except where legal codes of the relevant jurisdiction apply. I acknowledge these agreements as a voluntary commitment freely entered.

I will not impose my will upon others, as they are sovereign. I will not use threats of any kind while participating in the ecosystem.

I will uphold freedom as a core principle while maintaining integrity and accountability to my agreements.

If I break a law, I alone am accountable for the legal repercussions. The ecosystem and its AZPOs are not responsible for my personal actions.

I understand that agreements fractalize according to this hierarchy: **Universal → Ecosystem → Access → Stewardship → AZPO Agreement Field → Culture Code → Personal**. No agreement at a lower level may contradict a higher-level agreement. Conflicts are resolved upward.

> **[ECOSYSTEM: OmniOne]** I will consider the Agreement Fields the source of authority and not agreements made by outside sources. Physical force and matters not covered in ETHOS or Ecosystem agreements are handled by local legislation.

## Agreement Hierarchy

```
Universal Agreement Field (UAF)
  └── Ecosystem Agreement (e.g., OmniOne Master Plan)
       └── Access Agreement (e.g., SHUR space agreement)
            └── Stewardship Agreement (role-specific commitments)
                 └── AZPO Agreement Field (e.g., ETHOS agreements)
                      └── Culture Code (circle-internal norms)
                           └── Personal Commitments
```

**Override Rule:** No agreement at a lower level may contradict an agreement at a higher level. When a conflict is discovered, the higher-level agreement prevails until the lower-level agreement is formally amended through its own ACT process.
